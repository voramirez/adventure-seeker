Welcome to Dar es Salaam Coastal City

Discover Unique Street Food Places Through Social Media

When you travel to Tanzania, it will be a waste if you do not get a chance to try the local food. Food in restaurants don't necessarily represent the culture of the local people. If you want to explore delicious street food, then consider visiting Dar Es Salaam. One of my favorite place to eat when I land is sea food. I also love mishakis from the streets. Specific places I would suggest people to visit are Kariakoo, which has so many food places, my favorite thing to eat in Kariakoo is biryani. Other than that, feel free to explore Kigamboni, Posta, Bagamoyo and support local businesses as you do. To start making plans before traveling you can visit these instagram pages where they sell different types of street food:

  • Famous for its variety of sea food and beef but also has healthy options
  • Famous for its grilled delicacies

Beaches in Dar Es Salaam

My most favorite beaches in Dar Es Salaam are beaches in South Beach in Kigamboni, Coco Beach, Beaches in Bagamoyo and Mbudya Island. Most of these places are 2 to 1 hour away from the town center and they are very safe. Enjoy chilling with friends and enjoy the weekend.
